Online Job Search


The Online Job Search project aims to create a user-friendly web application that assists individuals in finding suitable job opportunities. This platform provides a convenient and centralized resource for job seekers to explore various job listings and connect with potential employers.

Key features of the Online Job Search project include:

  • Job search functionality: Users can search for jobs based on keywords, location, industry, and other relevant criteria.
  • Advanced filters: Users can apply filters to narrow down search results, such as salary range, job type, experience level, and more.
  • User profiles: Job seekers can create profiles to showcase their skills, experience, and qualifications to employers.
  • Job application management: Users can keep track of the jobs they have applied to and manage their applications.
  • Employer profiles: Companies and recruiters can create profiles to post job listings and provide information about their organization.
  • Notifications: Users can receive email or in-app notifications for new job listings, application updates, and other relevant information.

The Online Job Search project aims to streamline the job search process, making it easier for both job seekers and employers to connect and find the right opportunities or candidates. The intuitive user interface, powerful search capabilities, and personalized features make this platform a valuable resource in the job market.
